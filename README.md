## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the Superstore Sales dataset to evaluate business performance. The analysis is performed using Python in Google Colab, with an emphasis on identifying trends, high-performing products, and profitability.

---

## Objective

* Analyze overall business performance
* Identify revenue trends over time
* Determine top-performing products
* Evaluate profit distribution and patterns

---

## Dataset

**Superstore Sales Dataset**

The dataset includes:

* Order Date
* Sales
* Profit
* Product Name
* Category
* Region

---

## Analysis Tasks

### 1. Monthly Revenue Trends

* Aggregated sales data on a monthly basis
* Visualized revenue trends over time
* Identified growth patterns and seasonal variations

---

### 2. Top-Selling Products

* Analyzed product-wise sales
* Identified highest revenue-generating products
* Compared performance across categories

---

### 3. Profit Analysis

* Evaluated profit distribution
* Identified high-profit and low-profit areas
* Analyzed relationship between sales and profit
